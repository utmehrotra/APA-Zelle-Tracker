Zelle Payment Tracker

Effortlessly automate and track your Zelle payments in Google Sheets.

Features

  • Automatically categorizes Zelle payments by team or ID.
  • Tracks unidentified payments in a dedicated sheet.
  • Provides payment summaries by date and total amount.

Privacy and Data Usage

The Zelle Payment Tracker app securely processes your Gmail and Google Sheets data to track payments. We do not share or store your data outside your Google Workspace environment.
